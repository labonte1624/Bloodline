# Bloodline App Concept

Bloodline is a daily training companion app built around discipline, progression, and identity. The app turns workouts into a living system that adapts with the user over time.

## Product Vision
Create an experience where users feel guided, challenged, and accountable every day. The app should feel like a personal training system with a strong identity, not just a workout tracker.

## Core Experience
- Daily workout generation
- Progress tracking across weeks and months
- AI guidance from Anissa, the Viltrumite
- A persistent sense of growth and mastery

## Key Features
### 1. Daily Training Engine
- Generates a workout for the day based on goals, recovery, and prior performance
- Adjusts intensity and volume dynamically
- Supports strength, endurance, and skill-focused training

### 2. Progression System
- Tracks reps, sets, performance, and consistency
- Highlights streaks, plateaus, and breakthroughs
- Recommends next steps for improvement

### 3. Anissa Companion
- Acts as a coach, strategist, and accountability partner
- Delivers guidance in a sharp, disciplined tone
- Encourages commitment without fluff

### 4. Identity and Character Layer
- Users progress through themed halls or stages
- Each stage reflects a deeper commitment to the system
- The experience feels personal and symbolic, not just functional

## Core Screens
- Home: daily focus, streaks, and the current mission
- Workout: today’s plan, instructions, and completion tracking
- Progress: metrics, trends, and milestones
- Profile: identity, hall level, and achievements
- Settings: goals, preferences, and system configuration

## User Journey
1. User opens the app and sees the daily mission.
2. Anissa proposes or confirms the day’s training plan.
3. The user completes the workout and logs progress.
4. The app updates progression and recommends the next challenge.
5. The user continues building discipline over time.

## Product Tone
- Fierce but purposeful
- Disciplined rather than flashy
- Motivating without being soft

## Success Criteria
- Users return daily
- Training plans feel personal and adaptive
- Users feel stronger, more consistent, and more accountable
